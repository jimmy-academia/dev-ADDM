# Romantic Dining Suitability Assessment

Help assess how suitable this restaurant is for romantic dining (dates, anniversaries, proposals)
using customer reviews as evidence.
Assign a suitability label: Not Recommended, Acceptable, or Recommended.

## Definitions of Terms

### Incident

Consider reviews that mention any of the following:
- date nights, anniversaries, romantic occasions, proposals
- ambiance, lighting, noise level, privacy
- service style (rushed vs. attentive)
- overall romantic atmosphere

Reviews that only discuss food without mentioning atmosphere carry less weight.

### Account Type
How the reviewer relates to the described event

- **Firsthand**: The reviewer or their dining party experienced the event directly (e.g., "I had...", "my child...")
- **Secondhand**: The reviewer reports someone else's experience (e.g., "my friend...", "I heard...")
- **Hypothetical**: Concern or preference without describing an actual incident (e.g., "I'm allergic so I worry...")

### Date Outcome
How the date/romantic occasion went

- **Negative**: Date was negatively affected by restaurant experience
- **Neutral**: Restaurant didn't particularly help or hurt the date
- **Positive**: Restaurant contributed positively to the date
- **Memorable**: Exceptional experience, memorable occasion

### Ambiance Quality
Overall romantic ambiance assessment

- **Poor**: Not romantic at all - bright, loud, unromantic decor
- **Fair**: Some romantic elements but significant drawbacks
- **Good**: Pleasant romantic atmosphere, suitable for dates
- **Excellent**: Exceptional romantic setting, perfect for special occasions

### Noise Level
Noise level affecting intimate conversation

- **Loud**: Difficult to have conversation, must shout
- **Moderate**: Some background noise but conversation is possible
- **Quiet**: Easy to have private conversation
- **Intimate**: Very quiet, perfect for intimate conversation

### Lighting Quality
Lighting appropriateness for romantic dining

- **Harsh**: Bright overhead or fluorescent lighting
- **Neutral**: Standard restaurant lighting, not particularly romantic
- **Dim**: Subdued lighting, romantic feel
- **Candlelit**: Candles or very soft romantic lighting

### Service Style
Service style appropriateness for romantic dining

- **Rushed**: Staff rush the meal, no time to linger
- **Normal**: Standard service, neither rushed nor especially attentive
- **Attentive**: Attentive without being intrusive
- **Romantic**: Service enhances romantic experience (special touches)


## Verdict Rules

A restaurant is considered **Recommended** if any of the following is true:
- 4 or more reviews describe excellent romantic ambiance [filter: ambiance_quality=excellent]
- 15 or more reviews mention successful dates, proposals, or anniversaries [filter: date_outcome=positive]
- 4 or more reviews describe quiet or intimate noise level [filter: noise_level=['quiet', 'intimate']]

**Not Recommended** if Recommended does not apply, but any of the following is true:
- 4 or more reviews describe loud noise preventing conversation [filter: noise_level=loud]
- 3 or more reviews describe harsh lighting [filter: lighting_quality=harsh]
- 8 or more reviews describe negative date experiences [filter: date_outcome=negative]

**Acceptable** otherwise, especially when:
- Mixed reviews about romantic suitability
- Atmosphere is neutral - neither romantic nor anti-romantic
- Food is good but ambiance is not particularly romantic
