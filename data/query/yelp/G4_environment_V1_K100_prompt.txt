# Environment Quality Assessment

Help assess the physical environment quality at this restaurant using customer reviews as evidence.
This is from the perspective of a business owner evaluating ambiance and comfort.
Assign a quality label: Needs Improvement, Satisfactory, or Excellent.

## Definitions of Terms

### What to Consider (Guidance)

Consider reviews that mention any of the following:
- cleanliness of the space
- noise level
- seating comfort
- temperature comfort (AC, heating)
- ambiance and decor fit with concept

Reviews that only discuss food or service without mentioning environment carry less weight.

*Note: The above describes what types of content are relevant for this task. It is NOT a field to extract.*

### Account Type
How the reviewer relates to the described event

- **firsthand**: The reviewer or their dining party experienced the event directly (e.g., "I had...", "my child...")
- **secondhand**: The reviewer reports someone else's experience (e.g., "my friend...", "I heard...")
- **hypothetical**: Concern or preference without describing an actual incident (e.g., "I'm allergic so I worry...")

### Cleanliness
Cleanliness of the restaurant environment

- **dirty**: Visibly dirty, concerning
- **fair**: Could be cleaner but acceptable
- **clean**: Clean and well-maintained
- **spotless**: Exceptionally clean

### Noise Level
Noise level in the restaurant

- **deafening**: Extremely loud, cannot converse
- **loud**: Loud, conversation difficult
- **moderate**: Some noise but conversation possible
- **quiet**: Quiet, easy conversation

### Comfort
Physical comfort of seating/space

- **uncomfortable**: Seating or space is uncomfortable
- **fair**: Acceptable but not particularly comfortable
- **comfortable**: Comfortable seating and spacing
- **very_comfortable**: Exceptionally comfortable environment

### Temperature
Room temperature comfort

- **too_cold**: Uncomfortably cold
- **cold**: On the cold side
- **comfortable**: Temperature is comfortable
- **hot**: On the warm side
- **too_hot**: Uncomfortably hot

### Ambiance Fit
How well ambiance matches the concept

- **poor**: Ambiance doesn't fit the concept
- **fair**: Ambiance partially matches
- **good**: Ambiance matches concept well
- **excellent**: Ambiance perfectly complements concept


## Verdict Rules

A restaurant is considered **Excellent** if any of the following is true:
- 55 or more reviews praise excellent atmosphere
- 95 or more reviews describe it as spotless

**Needs Improvement** if Excellent does not apply, but any of the following is true:
- 15 or more reviews describe cleanliness issues
- 15 or more reviews report poor ambiance fit
- 9 or more reviews complain about deafening noise

**Satisfactory** otherwise, especially when:
- Environment is acceptable but unremarkable
- Some positive and some negative comments
- Basic expectations met but not exceeded
